ECOMMERCE SALES DASHBOARD

This Project uncovers actionable insights and optimize sales strategies through interactive data visualization

Project Overview: Ecommerce Sales Dashboard

This Ecommerce Sales Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the company sales performance, highlighting key metrics such as total revenue, profits, and order quantities. The analysis covers both global market performance and category-wise sales trends. The dashboard offers insights into the geographical distribution of market share, showcasing performance across five key regions: Pacific Asia, USCA, Europe, LATAM, and Africa.

With total sales of $14.11 million, a profit of $1.30 million, and 160,000 orders, the dashboard provides actionable insights that can guide strategic decisions. Regionally, Europe and LATAM lead the market, with 43.37% and 45.86% market shares respectively. The category-wise breakdown offers further insights into performance, with cardio equipment leading sales at $2.34 million, despite a decline of 4.05%. This analysis empowers stakeholders to optimize sales strategies, allocate resources effectively, and increase profitability.

Market Insights & Regional Breakdown

In this section, the dashboard provides detailed analysis on market share by sales across five regions. It highlights how each region influences customer acquisition, showing the interconnectivity and flow of customers from different geographical locations. This section provides valuable insights for market expansion and targeting the right demographics in each region. Finaly, It reveals how each region contributes to the overall sales and profit:

  • Pacific Asia: 8.32% of total market share
  • USCA (United States, Canada, and Mexico): 0.21% of total market share
  • Europe: 43.37% of total market share
  • LATAM (Latin America): 45.86% of total market share
  • Africa: 2.25% of total market share

Problem Statement & Proposed Solution

The ecommerce company faces challenges with declining sales in categories like fishing gear and women apparel, which have seen growth declines of -24.98% and -24.51%, respectively. Additionally, regions like Pacific Asia and Africa have smaller market shares compared to LATAM and Europe. To address these, the company should focus on two key strategies. First, category-specific recovery strategies, such as targeted marketing campaigns and product diversification, can help reverse the negative sales trends. Second, expanding market share in underperforming regions like Pacific Asia and Africa through localized marketing and customer acquisition efforts will balance the overall sales distribution and foster growth across all regions.
