Tesla Sales Dashboard SQL Breakdown (MySQL)

These SQL queries were used to extract and analyze Tesla global customer behavior, product performance and sales data, enabling the Power BI dashboard to deliver actionable insights for strategic decision-making and market expansion.

Total Sales Summary

This query aggregates the overall sales performance, showing the total number of customers, orders, and revenue across all Tesla markets.

Sales by Country

This query breaks down Tesla's sales by country, highlighting the regions that generate the most sales and orders.

Sales by Deal Size

This query provides insights into the distribution of Tesla orders based on deal size (small, medium, large), showing both the number of orders and total revenue per deal size.

Product Line Performance

This query highlights the sales performance of each Tesla product line, indicating which models have the highest order volume and revenue.

Underperforming Product Lines

This query identifies Tesla product lines with lower-than-average order volumes, suggesting areas for potential promotional efforts.
