Workforce Analytics Dashboard SQL Breakdown (MySQL)

These SQL queries were used to extract and analyze employee distribution, tenure, gender diversity, and retirement projections, providing valuable insights for workforce planning and HR decision-making through the Power BI dashboard.

Total Workforce Overview

This query aggregates the total number of employees, breaking them down by gender, and provides a snapshot of the workforce's size and gender distribution.

Employee Tenure Breakdown

This query shows the distribution of employee tenure, including how many employees have been with the organization for various lengths of time, with a focus on long-term employees.

Employees with 10+ Years of Service

This query highlights the employees who have been with the company for 10 or more years, indicating experienced personnel and potential retention strengths.

Retirement Forecast

This query identifies employees approaching retirement age (assuming 65 years), helping HR forecast the impact of retirements on workforce planning.

Employees Approaching Retirement

This query targets employees close to retirement (within the next 5 years), helping leadership prepare for knowledge transfer and succession planning.

Mid-Level Tenure Group

This query identifies mid-level tenure employees who are in the 6 to 9 years of service range, highlighting a key group for development and leadership pipeline growth.
