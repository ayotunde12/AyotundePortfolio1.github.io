Sheba Medical Center Dashboard SQL Breakdown

These SQL queries were used to extract and analyze healthcare financial and operational data, enabling the Power BI dashboard to deliver actionable insights for hospital decision-making.

Total Billing & Treatment Cost Summary

This query provides a complete financial overview of the hospital's revenue and cost structure. It shows the total billing amount and identifies major cost contributors like medications and room charges.

Insurance vs Out-of-Pocket Analysis

This query Breaks down the total payments received based on source—insurance or out-of-pocket. This insight helps in understanding the hospital’s reliance on insurance providers versus direct patient payments.

Top Insurance Providers by Billing

This query Identifies the top-performing insurance companies based on billing contributions. It helps evaluate partnerships and highlights which insurers contribute most to hospital revenue.

Top Performing Departments

This query Highlights the most profitable departments in the hospital. This helps in optimizing resource allocation and focusing on high-impact service areas.

Most Frequent Diagnoses

This query Lists the most commonly billed diagnoses along with their financial impact. This supports trend tracking in patient conditions and treatment needs.

Key Takeaway & Next Steps

This project expanded my expertise in healthcare data analytics, particularly in handling sensitive billing information and translating it into meaningful insights using excel, PowerBI & SQL. It demonstrates how business intelligence tools can directly support better healthcare administration. As a next step, I plan to integrate patient feedback metrics and real-time updates to further improve care quality and financial transparency.
