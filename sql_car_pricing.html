Car Price Analysis Dashboard SQL Breakdown

These SQL queries powered the insights in the Power BI dashboard, providing a transparent view into the pricing structure of Nigeria used car market.

Brand Frequency Overview

This query identifies the most frequently listed car brands in the dataset. It helps users recognize which brands dominate the Nigerian car market, indicating availability and potential resale demand.

Year vs. Average Price

This query Analyzes how car prices vary based on their year of manufacture. It reveals pricing trends over time and highlights which model years offer better value for money.

Engine Size & Pricing

This query Examines how engine size influences pricing by comparing average prices across various engine capacities. This helps buyers evaluate cost versus performance and fuel efficiency.

Mileage Sensitivity

This query Groups cars by mileage brackets to reveal how usage impacts market value. It helps buyers understand depreciation patterns and the price difference between low and high-mileage vehicles.

Brand Price Distribution

This query Summarizes price variations across different car brands. This query helps users evaluate the price range, identify premium brands, and spot those offering more affordability.
